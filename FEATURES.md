# THM Addons Features

This document lists the modules, HUD widgets, and utilities shipped with THM Addons. Module names match the in-game Meteor module list.

## Highway and Travel
- `THM-HighwayBuilder` ‚Äî Automatically builds highways according to THM standards.
- `THM Highway Monitor` ‚Äî Monitors alignment and recovers HighwayBuilder from drift. Requires Baritone.
- `Highway-Tools` ‚Äî Highway utilities: axis walker, highway teleporter, boundary finder, and highway checker. Requires Baritone.
- `axis-viewer` ‚Äî Displays world axes.
- `scaffold-THM` ‚Äî Scaffolds blocks under you.
- `auto-portal` ‚Äî For the Base Hunter who has places to be.
- `Fly-Bypass` ‚Äî Fly using packets.

## Mining and Building
- `tunnel-miner` ‚Äî Mines a tunnel block-by-block to target XZ coordinates at the same Y.
- `Speedmine` ‚Äî Mines blocks faster.
- `BlockCounter` ‚Äî Counts the selected blocks.
- `Better-echest-farmer` ‚Äî Better echest farmer that uses instant rebreak exploit.
- `sign-render` ‚Äî Renders sign text through walls with advanced clustering.

## Inventory and Utility
- `offhand-manager` ‚Äî Automatically manages your offhand (optimized for highway work).
- `hotbar-manager` ‚Äî Automatically move items to your hotbar.
- `chest-tracker` ‚Äî Track items in containers.
- `paket-limiter` ‚Äî Limits outgoing packets per tick with a bypass list.
- `AntiDrop` ‚Äî Stops you from dropping certain items.
- `auto-ignore` ‚Äî Auto-ignores Spambots and fake KitBots.
- `Kitbot-frontend` ‚Äî Send kitbot commands (`$update`, `$goto`, `$kit`).
- `discord-notifs` ‚Äî Sends notifications to a Discord webhook.
- `discord-RPC` ‚Äî Displays the THM Addon as an activity on Discord.
- `unfocused-fps` ‚Äî Limits the FPS and optionally sound when the game is unfocused or not the main task.
- `afk-logout` ‚Äî Logs out when you reach certain conditions. Useful for AFK travelling.

## PvP
- `auto-trap+` ‚Äî Traps a target player. Adds an optional anti-cheat friendly support placement mode.
- `surround-plus` ‚Äî Surrounds feet with Obsidian using strict logic.
- `phase` ‚Äî Allows player to phase through solid blocks using ender pearls.
- `AutoConcrete` ‚Äî Drops falling blocks above enemies' heads.
- `AntiConcrete` ‚Äî Places a button under yourself when enemies are nearby or dropping blocks above you.
- `AntiConcreteDetection` ‚Äî Breaks buttons and torches inside enemy hit-box.
- `AntiFeetPlace` ‚Äî Interrupts enemies FeetPlace with ender-chests.
- `armor-notify` ‚Äî Notifies you when your armor pieces are low.

## HUD Widgets
- `online-friends` ‚Äî Displays online friends from your friend list.
- `Dubs Count` ‚Äî Displays how many dubs are in render distance.
- `Highway-hud` ‚Äî View your stats while paving.
- `THM-welcomer` ‚Äî Advanced Welcomer.
- `crystal-metrics` ‚Äî ThunderHack style monitor.
- `THM Member Hud` ‚Äî Shows all online THM members and ranks.
- `KOS Hud` ‚Äî Shows all online Kill-on-Sight (KOS) players.
- `tunnel-miner-hud` ‚Äî Displays Tunnel Miner stats.
- `afk-logout-hud` ‚Äî Displays AFK Logout remaining time.

## Commands
- `center`
- `eclip`
- `desync`
- `uuid`

## UI
- Custom THM categories: `THM Highway` and `THM PVP`.
- GUI themes: `Dark`, `Snowy`, `Lambda`, `Stardust`, `Midnight`, `Monochrome`, `Nether`.
